How to Play

  • 🐾 Name your cat and care for them every day.
  • 🐱 Pet, feed milk, and walk your cat to boost their mood.
  • 💬 Keep your cat happy to see cute dialogue bubbles.
  • 🎀 Earn coins by interacting with your cat.
  • 🍓 Spend coins to buy desserts from the menu.
  • 🎒 Items go to your inventory—feed them anytime!
  • 🌈 Some foods give your cat special effects!
  • 🐾 Visit the Cat Status page to check mood and play!
